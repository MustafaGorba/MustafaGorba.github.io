About Us

We are a leading airline company that provides safe and comfortable flights to our passengers. Our team is dedicated to providing excellent customer service and ensuring that every flight is an enjoyable experience.

Who are we?

At ARA-Travel, we are more than just an airline; we're your gateway to seamless journeys and unforgettable experiences. Established in 1998 by aviation enthusiasts Alex Reynolds and Rachel Andrews, we've soared to new heights, connecting travelers worldwide with our extensive network of destinations. With a fleet of modern aircraft and a commitment to safety and reliability, we ensure that every flight with us is not just a trip, but an adventure. Whether you're jetting off for a business conference in bustling New York City or escaping to the serene beaches of Bali for a well-deserved vacation, ARA-Travel is here to make your travel dreams a reality.
